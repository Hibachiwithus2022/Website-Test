Our Story

Restaurant Quality.
Your Backyard.

Hibachi Connect was born from a simple belief: the best part of a hibachi restaurant isn't just the food — it's the show. The sizzle, the flying shrimp, the onion volcano. We bottle that energy and bring it directly to you, coast to coast.

Our chefs are trained teppanyaki professionals who blend culinary mastery with pure showmanship. Birthday, anniversary, corporate retreat, or just a Friday night with friends — we make it unforgettable, no matter where you are in the continental U.S.
