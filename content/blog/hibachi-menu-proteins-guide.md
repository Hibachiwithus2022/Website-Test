One of the best parts about booking a private hibachi chef is the ability to customize your menu. Every guest picks their own proteins, and you can add appetizers, noodles, and premium upgrades on top. Here's a complete breakdown of everything on the Hibachi Connect menu.

## Standard Proteins (Pick 2 Per Person)

Every guest selects 2 proteins from the standard list at no extra charge:

| Protein | Notes |
|---|---|
| **Chicken** | The crowd favorite — tender, well-seasoned |
| **Steak** | Sirloin, cooked to order |
| **Shrimp** | Jumbo shrimp, grilled with garlic butter |
| **Scallops** | Sea scallops, seared perfectly |
| **Salmon** | Fresh Atlantic salmon fillet |
| **Tofu** | Great for vegetarians, absorbs flavor beautifully |

## Premium Protein Upgrades

Want to upgrade your experience? These proteins are available at an added per-person cost:

| Protein | Upgrade Price |
|---|---|
| **Filet Mignon** | +$5 per person |
| **Lobster Tail** | +$10 per person |

Premium proteins count as one of your two protein selections. So you could do Filet Mignon + Shrimp, or Lobster Tail + Salmon — it's fully flexible.

## What's Always Included

Every event includes these alongside your proteins:

- **House salad** with ginger dressing
- **Fried rice** (egg fried rice, cooked tableside)
- **Grilled mixed vegetables** (zucchini, mushrooms, onions, bean sprouts)

## Sauces

Your chef brings a full sauce spread:

- **Yum Yum Sauce** (the classic, creamy hibachi sauce)
- **Ginger Sauce** (light and tangy)
- **Teriyaki Glaze**
- **Soy Sauce**

## Add-Ons

Want to build out your menu? These can be added to any event:

| Add-On | Price |
|---|---|
| Extra protein order (any standard) | $15/order |
| Extra Filet Mignon order | $20/order |
| Extra Lobster Tail order | $25/order |
| Yakisoba Noodles | $5/order |
| Gyoza (8 pieces) | $10/order |
| Edamame | $6/order |

Add-ons are priced per order, not per person. One order of Gyoza (8 pieces) typically feeds 4–6 guests as an appetizer.

## How to Build the Perfect Menu

**For a family party (kids + adults):**
- Adults: Chicken + Steak or Shrimp
- Kids: Chicken (most kid-friendly option)
- Add: Gyoza for the table, Yakisoba Noodles for the carb lovers

**For a bachelorette or upscale event:**
- Everyone: Filet Mignon + Lobster Tail (full premium upgrade)
- Add: Gyoza, Edamame, and extra Scallops

**For a corporate dinner:**
- Mix of proteins by individual preference
- Add: Gyoza and Edamame as passed appetizers during setup
- Optional: Premium proteins for VIP guests

**For vegetarians or dietary restrictions:**
- Tofu + Scallops, or Tofu + Salmon
- All sauces can be served on the side
- Let your chef know about any allergies in advance

## Allergy & Dietary Note

Hibachi cooking uses soy sauce, sesame oil, and shared cooking surfaces. **Cross-contamination is possible.** If anyone in your group has a severe allergy (shellfish, soy, gluten, sesame), please inform us in advance so your chef can take appropriate precautions.
