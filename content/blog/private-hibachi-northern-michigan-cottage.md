If you've rented a cottage on the Old Mission Peninsula, booked a resort property at Bay Harbor, or landed at a place on Little Traverse Bay with a terrace that looks out at the water, you already know the problem with driving to a restaurant for dinner: it breaks everything.

Northern Michigan's finest settings — [Traverse City](/locations/michigan/traverse-city) wine country, [Petoskey](/locations/michigan/petoskey) and Bay Harbor, the Harbor Springs harbor and Mackinac Island corridor — are the settings. The dinner should happen in them, not away from them.

Private hibachi at your Northern Michigan cottage or resort property brings a certified teppanyaki chef to your outdoor space with a propane teppan grill, hand-selected premium proteins, and a live-fire cooking performance that pairs as naturally with a 2022 Old Mission Riesling as it does with a Northern Michigan July sunset. You stay exactly where you are. The chef handles everything — including complete cleanup when dinner is done.

## Why Private Hibachi Works in Northern Michigan

Northern Michigan has some of the most beautiful outdoor entertaining settings in the Great Lakes region. The problem is that most restaurant options in Traverse City and Petoskey — while genuinely excellent — are built for walk-in guests, not for 20 people who happen to be staying at the same wine country estate for a week and want to eat together at dusk.

Private hibachi solves the logistics problem entirely. The chef comes to you. Your group doesn't move. The dinner happens at the terrace, deck, or cottage grounds where you're already gathered — with a live cooking performance that makes the occasion feel as extraordinary as the setting.

**What the chef brings:**
- Propane teppan grill (self-contained, no gas hookups required)
- Hand-selected proteins — premium upgrades including filet mignon, lobster tail, Chilean sea bass, and wagyu beef available and recommended for Northern Michigan occasions
- House-made sauces, fried rice ingredients, miso soup, garden salad
- Plates, chopsticks, serving utensils
- Complete setup and teardown — your property is immaculate when the chef leaves

**What you need:**
- A flat outdoor area of at least 10×10 feet on your terrace, deck, or cottage grounds
- Seating for your guests
- Vehicle access to the property (gated estates: provide code 24 hours ahead)

Setup takes 20 minutes. The performance runs 90–120 minutes. Cleanup is complete.

## Traverse City: Wine Country and Grand Traverse Bay Entertaining

[Traverse City](/locations/michigan/traverse-city) is Northern Michigan at its most refined and at its most vibrant simultaneously — the Cherry Festival and the Traverse City Film Festival that make July downtown an event, and the Old Mission Peninsula and Leelanau Peninsula wine country that makes the surrounding roads among the most beautiful in the Midwest.

The guests who book a Traverse City cottage or wine country rental have planned this trip carefully. They've mapped the Bowers Harbor and Black Star Farms tasting routes. They may have taken the ferry to Mackinac Island or spent an afternoon in Harbor Springs. The dinner at the property is the evening they want to get exactly right.

**The Old Mission Peninsula:** The narrow land that extends north into Grand Traverse Bay between East Bay and West Bay is one of Michigan's most exceptional wine-growing regions. The rental properties on Old Mission — some of which overlook the vineyard rows with East Bay on one side and West Bay on the other — are among the best hibachi settings we work in. Setup on the terrace or outdoor deck. The chef performs. The wine is already there.

**East Bay and West Bay properties:** Grand Traverse Bay waterfront cottages and resort properties are another strong Traverse City setting. The water view, the summer evening light, the group gathered at the grill on the deck above the bay. Premium proteins are the standard choice for Traverse City destination events.

**What Traverse City hosts book:**
- Milestone anniversary getaways (cottage or wine country rental, premium proteins, 8–16 guests)
- Bachelorette weekends (wine country theme continues into the evening with a private chef)
- Multi-family summer cottage week (large group dinner mid-week, the evening everyone plans around)
- Corporate retreat finale dinner (the team event that ends the business portion of the trip)

Traverse City summer events book 3–4 weeks ahead. Peak July and August fill fastest. The National Cherry Festival (usually the first or second week of July) makes that week particularly competitive — book 5–6 weeks ahead for Cherry Festival week.

## Petoskey and Bay Harbor: The Northern Michigan Resort Market

[Petoskey](/locations/michigan/petoskey) is the resort center of Northern Michigan's most exclusive summer community. Little Traverse Bay. The Bay Harbor marina and its estate properties. The Gaslight District's galleries and boutiques. Harbor Springs just to the north — the sailing families and the harbor-front estates that have been summer addresses for generations. Mackinac Island accessible by ferry from Mackinaw City, close enough to day-trip from a Petoskey cottage base.

The guests who are in Petoskey during the summer are genuinely discerning about the quality of their time here. The Bay Harbor estate property deserves a dinner that matches it. The Little Traverse Bay resort setting is the frame — the dinner should be worthy of it.

**What Petoskey and Bay Harbor hosts book:**

**Anniversary and milestone couples:** The Bay Harbor or Harbor Springs couple celebrating a significant occasion. Premium proteins — lobster tail and filet mignon together — and a chef who performs exclusively for your group at your property. The most common Petoskey booking profile.

**Estate and resort group dinners:** The family that has been coming to the same Little Traverse Bay cottage for thirty years, now gathering for a reunion with twenty-five people. A dual-station setup on the cottage grounds. Everyone at the grill at the same time.

**Corporate retreat groups:** The executive team at the Bay Harbor or Boyne City resort property. The team dinner at the end of the retreat that needs to be as impressive as everything else about the week.

**Harbor Springs and Mackinac Island visitors:** Guests who spent the day in Harbor Springs or came back from Mackinac Island ferry day and want the evening at the cottage to be the highlight. Private hibachi at the Petoskey property with premium proteins is the format that works.

Petoskey and Bay Harbor summer events book 3–4 weeks ahead. Remote Bay Harbor estate or Lake Charlevoix locations may have a travel fee — always disclosed upfront before you confirm.

## Premium Proteins for Northern Michigan Events

Northern Michigan destination events are where premium protein upgrades make the most sense. The setting is extraordinary. The occasion is planned. The dinner should match both.

**Recommended for Northern Michigan events:**
- **Lobster tail** — the most popular Northern Michigan upgrade. Pairs well with white wine and the lake setting.
- **Filet mignon** — consistently ordered by Traverse City wine country and Petoskey resort guests. The elegant choice.
- **Chilean sea bass** — delicate, mild, exceptional with ginger sauce. Popular with guests who want something different from the standard steak-and-shrimp format.
- **A5 Wagyu beef** — for events where nothing but the best is appropriate. Bay Harbor estate events most commonly.

You can mix and match proteins per guest — indicate preferences at booking. Premium packages are confirmed with your quote, no separate ordering needed.

## Booking Timeline

Northern Michigan summer is competitive. Here's how to approach it:

- **Peak July–August weekends (Traverse City and Petoskey):** Book 4–6 weeks ahead. July 4th week, the Cherry Festival week, and the last two weekends of August fill earliest.
- **June and September:** 3–4 weeks ahead. More availability than peak summer but still books out.
- **Corporate retreat groups:** Reach out 4–6 weeks ahead if you have a specific date. Retreat schedules are fixed — confirm early.
- **Anniversary/milestone events:** These are date-specific by nature. Book as early as you know the date.

Most Northern Michigan quotes are returned same day. Travel fees for remote cottage locations — always disclosed before you confirm, never a surprise.
