## Ocean City: The Bachelorette Weekend, Family Reunion, and Group Vacation Market

[Ocean City](/locations/maryland/ocean-city) runs from Memorial Day to Labor Day on group vacation energy. The bachelorette groups from Baltimore and DC that take over the Coastal Highway condos on summer weekends. The extended family reunions that rent the beach house on 94th Street because it's the only property big enough for thirty people. The corporate team that drove down from Northern Virginia on a Thursday for a long weekend and wants the beach dinner to be as memorable as the beach itself.

These groups share a common problem: restaurants in Ocean City are not designed for parties of more than eight or ten. The good waterfront restaurants have waits. The large group-friendly places are fine but not extraordinary. And the act of driving from the rental to the restaurant and back — navigating Coastal Highway traffic in July — uses up two hours that could have been spent on the deck.

**Private hibachi solves the group dinner problem at Ocean City:**

The chef travels to your vacation rental or beach house. Sets up on your deck, patio, or outdoor space. Performs for your entire group simultaneously — one chef for up to 20, two chefs for groups over 25. Every dish cooked to order in front of your guests. Fire tricks. Flying shrimp. The full teppanyaki performance that nobody in your group has seen done in their own vacation rental before.

When dinner is done: complete cleanup. Your rental is immaculate. The evening continues without any trace that someone just cooked for thirty people on the deck.

**What Ocean City private hibachi events look like:**

*Bachelorette weekends (the most common OC occasion):* 10–18 guests. Coastal Highway condo or north OC beach house. Saturday dinner before the evening out, or Sunday dinner as the trip's closing celebration. Standard proteins are excellent; king shrimp and scallops are particularly popular for bachelorette groups. Premium upgrades available.

*Family reunions:* 20–40 guests. Beach house with enough outdoor space for two chef stations. Dual-station setup for groups over 25 means nobody is waiting — the whole family eats together. The evening around the grill becomes the family reunion event that everyone references at next year's planning call.

*Vacation rental group dinners:* The mid-week group dinner for the friends who rented a house together for a week. Budget-flexible, occasion-flexible, format perfect for the setting.

## St. Michaels: The Milestone Occasion and Waterfront Cottage Market

[St. Michaels](/locations/maryland/st-michaels) is a different market than Ocean City entirely. Where Ocean City is volume and energy, St. Michaels is intention and occasion.

The Talbot County waterfront town — the Chesapeake Bay Maritime Museum, the harbor, the Main Street galleries and restaurants, the Miles River and the St. Michaels harbor — draws a specific kind of vacation guest: the couple celebrating something, the group that wants the Chesapeake Bay setting with genuine quality, the family that has been coming to the same cottage on Broad Creek every summer for twenty years and wants this year's dinner to be the one that marks the milestone.

**What St. Michaels private hibachi events look like:**

*Anniversary dinners (the most common St. Michaels occasion):* The couple who rented the waterfront cottage for their anniversary weekend. Lobster tail and filet mignon — the most common St. Michaels protein combination. A chef who performs for just the two of them, or for the six couples who came together to mark the occasion. The Miles River at sunset. A dinner worth the drive from DC.

*Wine country weekends:* St. Michaels and the Eastern Shore wine scene have grown significantly — the groups that come to the Shore for wine tasting also want the Saturday dinner to be something the evening is organized around. Private hibachi at the rental fits the format: the chef arrives while the group is finishing the afternoon's last bottle, and dinner is the natural next thing.

*Family cottage events:* The family that has been coming to the same St. Michaels property for decades, now gathering for a reunion. Thirty people at the cottage grounds, dual chef stations, the whole family at the same grill at the same time.

**Kent Island and the surrounding Eastern Shore:**

## What Premium Proteins Mean for a Shore Vacation Event

Premium proteins are the most common upgrade at Maryland shore events, and for good reason: the setting is already extraordinary, and the dinner should match it.

**For Ocean City vacation events:**
- **King shrimp** — the most popular OC upgrade. The bachelorette group that went deep-sea fishing earlier and wants the evening's food to match the day's theme.
- **Scallops** — delicate, mild, excellent with ginger sauce. Popular with groups that want something beyond the standard steak-and-shrimp format.
- **Lobster tail** — the full occasion upgrade. Family reunion events where everyone gets the full premium experience.

**For St. Michaels and Chesapeake Bay cottage events:**
- **Lobster tail + filet mignon** — the most requested St. Michaels combination. The anniversary dinner on the Miles River. The couple who wants the best.
- **Chilean sea bass** — pairs naturally with the Chesapeake Bay waterfront setting. Mild, flaky, genuinely exceptional.
- **A5 wagyu** — for the milestone occasion where nothing less than the best is appropriate.

Premium proteins are mixed and matched per guest at booking. You choose at the time of confirmation — no separate ordering, no additional logistics.

## Booking Timeline for Maryland Shore Events

The Eastern Shore summer is competitive. Here's how to approach it:

**Ocean City peak weekends (July–August):** Book 4–6 weeks ahead. Holiday weekends (July 4th, Labor Day) fill earliest. If you're planning a July bachelorette weekend, start the booking process when the trip dates are confirmed.

**St. Michaels summer weekends:** Book 3–4 weeks ahead. Waterfront cottage properties with smaller outdoor spaces should be confirmed early. Let us know the deck or patio dimensions at booking.

**Kent Island and Eastern Shore:** Same timeline as St. Michaels. Travel fees for remote island or waterfront locations — always disclosed before confirmation.

**June and September:** Better availability than peak summer. 2–3 weeks ahead is typically sufficient. The shoulder season events at OC and St. Michaels tend to have the most relaxed booking environment.
