Here is the problem with planning a group dinner at Myrtle Beach.

[Myrtle Beach](/locations/south-carolina/myrtle-beach) in July — any July — has a two-hour wait at every restaurant on the Oceanfront that's worth going to. The bachelorette group of fourteen that wants to eat together discovers that the good restaurants don't accommodate parties over eight without a private dining room reservation that was booked in March. The family reunion with twenty-two people in the beach house finds that moving everyone to a restaurant means navigating Ocean Boulevard traffic, finding parking for five cars, splitting a group that doesn't want to be split, and spending ninety minutes doing logistics that nobody wants to manage on a vacation.

The solution is the same one that groups at Nashville rentals and Gatlinburg cabins have been using: the chef comes to the vacation rental.

Mobile hibachi at your [Myrtle Beach](/locations/south-carolina/myrtle-beach) or [North Myrtle Beach](/locations/south-carolina/north-myrtle-beach) vacation rental changes everything. The group never leaves the property. The chef sets up on the deck, pool area, or backyard, performs for 90–120 minutes, and leaves the rental spotless. The dinner becomes the anchor of the trip — the evening the group is still referencing on the drive back to Charlotte or Columbia or Raleigh.

## Why Vacation Rentals Are the Right Setting

Myrtle Beach vacation rentals have outdoor space designed for exactly this kind of gathering. The oceanfront condo at 71st Street with the deck that faces the Atlantic. The North Myrtle Beach house in Cherry Grove with the pool and the patio and enough seating for twenty people without trying. The large beach house rental on Ocean Boulevard that the family chose because it was the only one big enough for the reunion.

These properties were designed for groups. Private hibachi is designed for groups. The pairing works in a way that a reservation at a Strand restaurant doesn't.

What makes the mobile hibachi setup work at any Myrtle Beach rental:

**Fully self-contained.** The chef brings a propane teppan grill — no gas hookup at the vacation rental is needed. No outdoor kitchen required. The setup is designed for exactly this kind of property. If the deck has 10×10 feet of clearance, the chef can cook there.

**The group stays together.** For a bachelorette group of fifteen, a family reunion of twenty-five, or any gathering larger than eight: private hibachi is the format that keeps everyone together. One performance, one grill, one experience — everyone eating at the same time, watching the same show, at the same pace.

**No transportation coordination.** No Ubers. No parking. No splitting the group because the restaurant can't seat everyone together. The group is already at the rental. The chef comes to the rental. The evening ends at the rental.

**Complete cleanup.** When dinner is done, the chef breaks down the setup and takes everything with them. The deck is left exactly as it was. The vacation rental is spotless.

## Myrtle Beach: Bachelorette Weekends and Group Vacations

[Myrtle Beach](/locations/south-carolina/myrtle-beach) is one of the top bachelorette destinations on the East Coast — and private hibachi has become one of the most popular dinner formats for groups who have discovered it.

The typical Myrtle Beach bachelorette hibachi event:

**Group size:** 12–18 guests is the most common bachelorette format. For groups over 25, we bring two chefs and two teppan stations running simultaneously so everyone eats at the same time.

**Setting:** The vacation rental deck, pool area, or outdoor space. Oceanfront properties, pool deck condos, and backyard houses all work — the chef confirms the setup at booking.

**Timing:** Most bachelorette groups book the hibachi dinner as the anchor evening activity — starting at 5:30 or 6:00 PM, giving the group an extraordinary dinner experience before heading out for the rest of the night. The performance runs 90–120 minutes.

**Proteins:** The standard menu includes chicken, steak, shrimp, salmon, and vegetables. Bachelorette groups often add premium upgrades — filet mignon and lobster tail are the most popular choices. Confirm at booking.

**What to do after dinner:** The chef has cleaned up and left before you need to decide. The group is already dressed, already in the vacation rental, already on the same schedule. The evening continues exactly where you want it to go.

## North Myrtle Beach: Family Reunions and Quieter Vacation Groups

[North Myrtle Beach](/locations/south-carolina/north-myrtle-beach) has a different character from the Oceanfront corridor of Myrtle Beach — quieter, more residential-feeling, the Cherry Grove and Crescent Beach communities that attract family reunion groups, multi-generational vacation parties, and groups who chose the northern end of the Strand specifically because it's less crowded.

For family reunions, private hibachi at the North Myrtle Beach house is often the best dinner format available. The group that has twenty-two people across three generations can't all fit comfortably at a restaurant together. The grandparents don't want to navigate the Ocean Boulevard traffic. The kids are already happy at the beach house. The solution that keeps everyone together and delivers an extraordinary dinner is the chef at the property.

**Family reunion considerations:**
- Guest counts of 20–40 are common for reunion events — dual stations for groups over 25
- Multi-generational groups have diverse protein preferences — the standard menu covers everyone, and kids' portions are straightforward
- The performance works for all ages — fire tricks and flying shrimp are crowd favorites whether the audience is eight or eighty
- Plan dual stations early if you expect more than 25 guests — this can't be arranged last-minute

## Pawleys Island: The Quieter Coastal Market

[Pawleys Island](/locations/south-carolina/pawleys-island) sits about 20 miles south of Myrtle Beach and offers a completely different character. The oldest resort town on the East Coast. The historic beach cottages. The families who have been coming to the same Pawleys house for twenty or thirty years.

Private hibachi at a Pawleys Island property tends to be occasion-specific. The anniversary couple who chose the beach cottage because the setting is extraordinary and the dinner should match it. The family that uses Pawleys as the base for a late-summer week and wants the Saturday dinner to be the event the whole trip is organized around. The milestone birthday celebration at the waterfront property.

Pawleys Island events book 3–4 weeks ahead in peak summer. For holiday weekends (Fourth of July, Labor Day), book earlier.

## Booking a Grand Strand Hibachi Event

**When to book:** 
- Peak summer (June–August) and holiday weekends: 4–6 weeks ahead
- Shoulder season (April–May, September–October): 2–3 weeks
- Off-season events: 1–2 weeks is usually sufficient

**What you need at booking:**
- The vacation rental address (so we can confirm deck setup and calculate any travel logistics)
- Guest count (and whether you expect over 25, which requires two chef stations)
- Date and start time
- Protein preferences (standard menu or premium upgrades)

**After booking:** Most Grand Strand quotes are returned same day. Confirm with a deposit and your date is locked. The chef will reach out 24–48 hours before the event with arrival details and confirm any access notes for the rental.
