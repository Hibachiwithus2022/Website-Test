## The Chesapeake Bay Waterfront Setting: What Works Best

Most [Annapolis](/locations/maryland/annapolis) and [Severna Park](/locations/maryland/severna-park) waterfront properties have ideal private hibachi setups. Here's what we need:

**Space:** A flat outdoor area of at least 10×10 feet. Dock-side decks, rear terraces, and waterfront patios all work. Pool decks work if there's clearance from the water.

**Access:** Vehicle access to unload the grill and supplies. For gated communities, provide the access code 24 hours before the event. For properties accessed by boat, contact us to discuss logistics.

**No hookups needed:** The teppan grill runs on self-contained propane. No gas connections at your property required.

**Wind:** Chesapeake Bay waterfront properties can be windy in the late afternoon. We account for this in setup positioning. The grill performs in moderate wind conditions.
